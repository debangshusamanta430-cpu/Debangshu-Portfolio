About Me

Who I Am

I'm Debangshu Samanta, a passionate video editor who believes every frame should serve the story. My core focus is storytelling — building a visual narrative that captures attention from the first second and keeps viewers hooked till the very end. Whether it's a short-form reel or a full documentary-style video, I approach every project as a story waiting to be told.

🎯 My Editing Philosophy: Great editing is invisible — the viewer shouldn't notice the cuts, they should just feel the emotion. I combine pacing, music, color, and motion to create videos that don't just look good but perform — they engage, convert, and connect.

My Process

How We Work Together

Simple, transparent, stress-free

1
Brief & Discussion
You share your idea, references, and goals. I ask the right questions to understand exactly what you need — not just what you said, but what you mean.
2
Footage & Asset Collection
You send me the raw footage, music, voiceover, or any other elements. I organize and review everything before starting.
3
Editing & Story Crafting
I build the narrative structure first, then layer in cuts, motion graphics, color grading, and sound design for a polished, story-driven result.
4
First Draft Delivery
You receive the first version for review. I walk you through the key decisions I made and why — so feedback is focused and clear.
5
Revisions & Final Delivery
We refine until it's exactly right. Final file delivered in your required format — ready to upload and perform.
